Our Historied Story

From the ancient origins of our craft to the modern manifestations of sartorial excellence, every garment encapsulates centuries of history and culture. From the Dunes of Arabia to the Terrains of Coorg

Legend whispers of our intrepid ancestors—Arab traders who cast their sails centuries ago. They were drawn by the allure of uncharted horizons along the verdant shores of southern India. Their voyage led them to the lush landscapes of Kodagu, where the seeds of what would burgeon into the Chadkhan legacy were sown.

Ancient Luxury to Meet Rising Needs

In an era where bustling shopping hubs were but figments of imagination and travelling through posed formidable challenges, our forebears erected modest shanties. These humble abodes were nestled amidst the heartlands of communities and served as sanctuaries of style. They beckoned villagers to explore the allure of fine garments. Thus, Chadkhan emerged as a bridge between necessity and luxury, offering the finest fabrics at the doorstep of patrons.

The Rise of Chadkhan

As time's loom wove its intricate tale, a new generation, led by the unwavering Mohammed Umar, inherited the stewardship of Chadkhan. Under his guiding light, our humble abode blossomed into a sanctuary of elegance amidst the lively streets of Coorg. Our first emporium rose, giving way to a legacy that still breathes in tradition.

Understanding Needs Became the Chadkhan Forte

From the grandeur of bridal attire to the simplicity of everyday wear, from the dignity of school uniforms to the magnificence of ceremonial robes, Chadkhan's emerged as a symbol of sartorial brilliance—a haven for life's myriad moments, from the joyous cradle to the solemn grave.

Leaping Toward a Glorius Future

Today, our legacy stands tall and proud, embodied in five sprawling emporiums that serve as a testament to our enduring commitment to tradition. In each garment offered by Chadkhan, echoes of our storied past reverberate—a testament to craftsmanship honed over generations, and a celebration of the artistry that defines us. As we navigate the ever-evolving currents of time, our vision remains steadfastly fixed on the horizon, where new adventures await, and new chapters in the saga of Chadkhan are yet to unfold.
